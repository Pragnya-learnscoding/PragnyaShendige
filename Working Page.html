Izmenyat

Izmentyat

Izmenyat is an immersive and strategic tabletop game set against the backdrop of the October Revolution of 1917. The game is targeted for the age group of 13-16 and uses the MDA framework of game design.

Mother Clucker

Card Game Design | Concept Design | Storytelling

Mother Clucker

Can’t you tell? It’s a mother clucking card game! My best friend and I gave 'Exploding Kittens' a desi makeover with partying Murgis. It’s a reverse deck-building compact box of endless entertainment you can play in a cozy game night with friends.
This card game project involved concept design, storytelling, and visual creativity. The objective was to create an engaging and humorous experience for players.
