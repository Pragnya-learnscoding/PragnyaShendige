Izmenyat

Board Game Design | Concept Design | Storytelling

Duration: 4 Weeks | 2024
Team of 5

Izmenyat is an immersive and strategic tabletop game set against the backdrop of the October Revolution of 1917. The game is targeted for the age group of 13-16 and uses the MDA framework of game design.

A Win

I am grateful to report that our team was one of the 25+ chosen to participate in TTOX’24. We had the privilege of networking and received positive feedback from numerous children who expressed their interest in playing our game.

Why Educational?

As Indian citizens and as high school graduates, we always observed the teaching in our education system did not always meet it’s mark. So decided to try a different approach, an approach that makes children visualise the war, it’s political strategy, causes and impact.

Why History?

History is a subject full of stories and events that have proven difficult for students to learn. Moreover, most students choose to learn Science or Commerce over Social Studies after their 10th grade, and we wanted to know why. Hence, we have chosen the chapters in History of Class 9 and 10.

We circulated an initial survey that listed down Chapters from class 9 and 10 respectively, and asked students to choose what they found the toughest among them in the respective classes. Finally, we asked students to choose the toughest chapter from both classes, and yet, class 9 chapter ‘Socialism in Europe and the Russian Revolution’ won the majority vote.

Game Overview?

At the game's outset, players are secretly assigned to either the White Army or the Red Army, with roles unveiled later. Players distribute resource tokens following the action card's order, gaining allies until they draw a fellowship card. During this critical phase, chance determines ally gain or loss. After special events conclude, eliminated players, based on acquired allies, enter a bidding round, hiding manifestos in the White Palace.

To win, players must correctly guess the October manifesto combination of resource tokens; failure results in victory for the White Army or neither teams depending on the total number of players.

Game Objective

Every player has a secret affinity to the red army or the white army Players on the Red army win if they guess the right manifesto and combination Players on the White army win when:

  • There are even number of players in the end and the red army guesses the right manifesto and combination
  • There are odd number of players and nobody finds the manifesto
